Arnold Dumbbell Press

Targeted muscle group: Shoulders

Description:

This exercise is used to strengthen the shoulders. It is named after Arnold Schwarzenegger, who used this move to help improve his shoulder development.

Tips:

1. Keep your back straight throughout the exercise.
2. Do not use your legs or torso to lift the weights; your shoulders should do all the work.
3. Perform the exercise slowly and with control, lowering the weights more slowly than you lift them.
4. Do not use weights that are too heavy; it is better to use lighter weights and perform the exercise correctly.
